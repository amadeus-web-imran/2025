Key aspects of spiritual troubleshooting include:

*   **Identifying the root cause:** It goes beyond simply acknowledging a feeling of distance or discontent. It involves asking specific questions to pinpoint the source of the problem. For example, is the struggle related to a conflict with one's higher power, internal doubts about beliefs, or issues within a religious community?
*   **Applying a systematic approach:** The process involves a step-by-step examination of different areas of spiritual life, much like checking a device's power source, connections, and settings. *   **Seeking solutions:** Once the problem is identified, spiritual troubleshooting aims to find and apply solutions. These solutions can vary widely depending on the nature of the struggle. *   **Recognizing different types of struggles:** Research in psychology and religion has identified various categories of spiritual struggles, including:
    *   **Divine struggles:** Feeling anger, abandonment, or punishment from a higher power.
    *   **Internal struggles:** Conflicts about one's own beliefs, values, or sense of purpose.
